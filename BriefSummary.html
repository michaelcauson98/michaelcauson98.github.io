A Brief Summary

The plot below is an approximate density histogram for the number of citizens killed by each of the 100 police departments, broken down by race from 2013-2019 (inclusive). A quick look shows us that, in general, BAME citizens are killed in larger numbers across the 100 departments compared to white citizens. In fact, it seems that the probability of a department killing 0 white citizens is over double that of BAME citizens.

Perhaps this is a little misleading since this plot hasn’t been scaled by population size. A US Census estimated that 60.4% of the US population are ‘White alone, not Hispanic nor Latino’. In other words, even if we scaled this by number of citizens of each race, say deaths per hundred thousand people, the characteristics of the above plot are only amplified.
